🧭

How It Works

Three steps from confusion
to conviction.

01
🗺️
Choose a Path to Explore
The student selects a career direction: Job, or Entrepreneurship. No pressure. No consequences. They can explore both. The simulation begins with a realistic entry point, not a summary of the path.
02
🎮
Experience Scenarios, Not Slides
Students navigate realistic situations from that path: a difficult day at work, a recruiter's shortlist decision, a founder's first product failure, an MBA application reality check. They feel the pressure and the reward of each choice.
03
🧭
Receive a Personalised Skill Roadmap
At the end, Career Explorer generates a skill gap analysis and a prioritised roadmap. Every tool in EduPodX that follows - GD Coach, VoiceUp, ProWrite, ALR, Skill Verification - is now directed toward a goal the student chose for themselves.
